Welcome to Tutoring Joy

We are Tutoring Joy, a tutoring group with an aim towards helping kids excel academically. We provide extra support for children who are struggling with class content, and are struggling to get the assistance that they need from their schools. We are also here for those who desire some additional curricular input outside of school hours.

Our Offered Packages

We have a very wide selection of different packages. We offer tutoring coming in one-on-one sessions or group sessions, with no more than 8 pupils in a group. One-on-one tuition is done at the home of the pupil, with the express presence of at least one guardian/responsible adult. Our group sessions are normally held in hired rooms from various venues, which would be confirmed upon booking.


Our tutoring is perfect for children of primary and high school, ranging from ages 5-16. We offer assistance in academic work, homework, and giving extra academic support for children who struggle with things learned in class. We also offer special packages to help with SATs and GCSE preparation for those pupils sitting these exams.
